FAQ

Are recruiters allowed on this site?

No. We don't want recruiters to be able to post here because they take away from your income, the company's money, and complicate the process.


Do I have to pay anything to sign up?

Jr Developers looking for Jobs don't have to pay a dime. We ask companies to pay a small fee per job to post, but this is significantly less than what they would pay a recruiter. This saves money for both parties and expedites the process.


Can I communicate with a developer/company if I want to get more information?

Absolutely. Our messaging system allows developers and companies to have private communciations if they want to learn more about each other.


How can I get my password/username if I forgot it?

You can click the forgot link on the login page and we will email you your information to the email account listed.


Will my resume be visible to companies?

Yes. Companies can search resumes by keywords to find developers that match their needs.


Can I save jobs or searches for later reference?

Certainly. There is a save function listed for companies and developers to save jobs they applied to, developer's profiles they viewed, etc.
